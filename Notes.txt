Phase 1 completed successfully.

The raw customer support tickets dataset was downloaded from Hugging Face and stored in the bronze layer. The dataset contains 61,765 rows and 16 columns. Python-based profiling reports and SQL-based profiling outputs were generated to understand schema, missing values, duplicates, categorical distributions, text length patterns, queue workload, priority distribution, and business field gaps.

The dataset is suitable for analytics and AI use cases, but enterprise operational fields such as ticket_id, status, created_date, resolved_date, resolution_time_hours, agent_name, sla_status, sentiment, and ticket_category need to be engineered in Phase 2.
